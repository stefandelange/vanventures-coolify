After the hike, we followed the irresistible smell of freshly baked waffles drifting through the air. This led us straight to a tiny knitting shop run by a group of elderly ladies who served us warm Norwegian waffles with jam. They were pure heaven ‚Äì and a nice vegetarian treat in a country where fish and meat tend to dominate the menu. Norwegians certainly know how to make waffles: soft, buttery and just sweet enough.

As the sun slowly sank towards the horizon, the sky changed colour by the second, from soft pink to glowing orange to deep purple. Every moment felt like a painting coming to life. We sat by the water, completely enchanted. Without question, it was the best sunset of our trip so far ‚Äì no music, no plans, just nature putting on her own show.
